Progress Report for MBR-VibClean Innovation:

Prototype Development:

A functional prototype of the MBR-VibClean system has been developed and tested in controlled environments. This step allowed us to evaluate the integration of the waterflow monitor, hydro-turbine, and magnetically induced membrane vibrator. Membrane Surface Cleaning Efficiency: Validation studies focused on quantifying the membrane surface cleaning efficiency. We compared the performance of MBR-VibClean with traditional cleaning methods to ensure a superior and reliable solution.

Membrane Surface Cleaning Efficiency:

Membrane Surface Cleaning Efficiency: Validation studies focused on quantifying the membrane surface cleaning efficiency. We compared the performance of MBR-VibClean with traditional cleaning methods to ensure a superior and reliable solution.

User Feedback and Iteration:

Early versions of the prototype were subjected to user feedback, enabling us to incorporate valuable insights into the design and functionality. This iterative process ensured that the final product meets the practical needs and expectations of end-users.

Progress in Developing the Innovation:

Technological Advancements: The MBR-VibClean technology has undergone significant advancements during the development phase. Improvements in the hydro-turbine design, waterflow monitoring algorithms, and membrane vibrator efficiency have contributed to the overall robustness of the system.

Energy Conversion Optimization:

Progress has been made in optimizing the energy conversion efficiency of the hydro-turbine. This involves fine-tuning the turbine to harness maximum kinetic energy from water flow, ensuring sustainable and cost-effective membrane cleaning.

Real-Time Monitoring Integration:

We have successfully integrated real-time monitoring features into the system, allowing for immediate detection of optimal cleaning times.
